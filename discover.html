Welcome to Simple Store Manager

Start getting your business on track with this web app for an easy and efficient way to manage your store, track inventory, and streamline your product listings.

Our powerful store management platform is designed to help businesses of all sizes stay on top of their inventory, manage products and orders, and track sales and profits. With our easy-to-use interface and robust features, you can keep track of every aspect of your store's operation, from product listings and customer orders to shipping and fulfillment.

Get Product Insights

This feature is designed to help people make informed decisions about which products to buy or sell, by displaying real-time or historical sales data for a given product.

Overall, this feature provides insights on product sales velocity that can be a valuable for anyone looking to buy or sell products, as it can help you make more informed decisions based on data and trends rather than just intuition or guesswork.
